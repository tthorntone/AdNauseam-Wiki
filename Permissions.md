AdNauseam's required permissions are based on those of uBlock. These are AdNauseam's required permissions:
```
"permissions": [
    "contextMenus",
    "privacy",
    "storage",
    "tabs",
    "unlimitedStorage",
    "webNavigation",
    "webRequest",
    "webRequestBlocking",
    "management",
    "<all_urls>",
],
```
